Launching Soon

What's inside

Everything you need to control your product data. Nothing you don't.

⚙️

Parts Management

Part numbers, names, revisions, part levels, and 10+ custom attributes — searchable and filterable.

🏗️

Assemblies & BOMs

Build parent/child assembly trees, view recursive indented BOMs, and run Where-Used queries instantly.

📊

Excel BOM Export

Download clean, indented BOMs as .xlsx — ready for purchasing, quoting, or the shop floor.

📁

Document Control

Attach drawings, CAD files, specs, and datasheets to parts — with automatic file versioning.

🔒

Check-In / Check-Out

Advisory locks tied to the logged-in user keep two engineers from stepping on the same part.

✅

Release Workflow

Move parts from Prototype to Released. Released parts lock against edits — reversible by role.

🔧

CAD-Aware Versioning

Native handling for .prt, .asm, .step, .stl, .sldprt and more — with open-in-place support on LAN.

👥

Roles & Access

Role-based permissions with local accounts or Google sign-in. You control who can view, edit, and release.

⚡

Fast & Self-Contained

Modern web stack, no heavyweight install, no per-seat enterprise pricing games. It just runs.

Built by engineers, for engineers.
HYPERPLM grew out of a real workshop's need to track real parts — not a sales deck. The full platform is in active development.

Product Lifecycle Management,

HYPERPLM is a fast, lightweight PLM for race teams and builders who outgrew spreadsheets in the trailer. Every part on the car, every BOM, every drawing and revision — tracked from the shop to the grid.

Launching Soon

What's inside

Everything you need to control your product data. Nothing you don't.

⚙️

Parts Management

Every component on the car — part numbers, revisions, and custom attributes. Know exactly what's bolted on, and what's on the shelf.

🏗️

Assemblies & BOMs

Full-car build trees: corner assemblies, drivetrain, aero. Recursive BOMs and instant Where-Used — which cars run that spindle?

📊

Excel BOM Export

Download clean, indented build sheets as .xlsx — ready for ordering spares, the trailer pack list, or tech inspection.

📁

Document Control

Attach drawings, CAD files, setup sheets, and dyno results to parts — with automatic file versioning.

🔒

Check-In / Check-Out

Advisory locks tied to the logged-in user keep two people from reworking the same part between race weekends.

✅

Release Workflow

Move parts from Prototype to Released. What's on the car is locked and known-good — reversible by role when development continues.

🔧

CAD-Aware Versioning

Native handling for .prt, .asm, .step, .stl, .sldprt and more — open designs in place right from the shop network.

👥

Roles & Access

Role-based permissions with local accounts or Google sign-in. Crew chief, fabricator, driver — everyone sees what they need.

⚡

Fast & Self-Contained

Modern web stack, no heavyweight install, no per-seat enterprise pricing games. Built to be as quick as what it tracks.

Born in the paddock, not a boardroom.
HYPERPLM grew out of a real race program's need to track real parts between real race weekends. The full platform is in active development.
